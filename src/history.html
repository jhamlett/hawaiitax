About HiAccounting

Hawaii Accounting LLC (HiAccounting) is a division of The Hawaii Group (HiGroup), Hawaii’s leading business process outsourcing firm. HiAccounting was founded in 2009 and is a certified public accounting firm based in Honolulu, Hawaii. We are specialized in providing accounting and tax services solutions customized to meet an organization’s specific needs.

The HiAccounting Advantage

The HiAccounting Advantage is providing local insight, technical acumen and a deep commitment to the community in which we serve in to be Hawaii's premier accounting and tax firm. Our clients chose HiAccounting because of our unique service model and a team of highly experienced and qualified professionals committed to ensuring that you receive quality service on your island. We strongly believe in working personally and directly with our clients, connecting through HiGroup’s core principles of integrity, transparency, and dedicated customer service.

HiAccounting’s comprehensive finance, accounting, reporting, budgeting, and tax services provide Hawaii organizations with professional accounting without the overhead and management challenges associated with building and maintaining an in-house accounting department. Businesses working with HiAccounting can free themselves of the demands of staffing and overseeing finance, accounting, and taxes.

We provide a welcome alternative to large local accounting firms or faceless offshore resources which for years have left Hawaii clients wanting more — more attention, more value, more reliability and more peace of mind. HiAccounting fills this void in the marketplace with experienced, professional, reliable local experts you know and trust.
