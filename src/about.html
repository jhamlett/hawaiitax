About HiAccounting

Hawaii Accounting LLC (HiAccounting) is a division of The Hawaii Group (HiGroup), Hawaii’s leading business process outsourcing firm. HiAccounting was founded in 2009 and is a certified public accounting firm based in Honolulu, Hawaii. We are specialized in providing accounting and tax services solutions customized to meet an organization’s specific needs.

Benefits of Outsourcing Your Accounting Services

Why outsource your finance department?

Outsourcing bookkeeping, accounting and finance is a growing trend among middle market and high-growth companies, providing a cost-effective way for organizations to improve the finance and accounting function. The benefits of outsourcing include fractional use of accounting professionals, improved processes and access to new technologies. Outsourcing allows organizations to scale resources to their needs with no obligation to pay salaries or benefit costs. Working with HiAccounting will help alleviate the burden of hiring, training and maintaining accounting staff.

Organizations can take advantage of advanced technology platforms to gain real-time visibility into their businesses.
